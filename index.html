About me:

Dimitrie Panainte holds an engineering degree in Computers and Information Technology from "Gheorghe Asachi" Technical University of Iaşi, Romania.
Since high school he liked to participate in competitions. As a student, in 2018, his team won 3rd prize at NXP CUP EMEA FINALS.
Video of the competition.
Since 2007, he has worked as a programmer for Continental Automotive Romania, which is a German multinational automotive parts manufacturing company for the automotive and transportation industries.
Later, he continues to work for Infineon Technologies Romania where he designs and develops automated tests for Infineon Security & Smart Cards.
Driven by curiosity and finding an opportunity, he travels to Osnabrück, Germany and he moves to Timisoara, Romania, where he works for Amazonen-Werke H. Dreyer GmbH & Co. KG which is a German manufacturer of Agricultural machinery and municipal machinery.
When not programming, Dimitrie enjoys skating, travelling, swimming and playing games.
